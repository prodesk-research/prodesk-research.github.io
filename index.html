About Pro Desk

Systematic F&O Research · India

Pro Desk publishes systematic, data-driven research on Indian index and F&O derivatives. Every morning brief, evening debrief, and weekly summary is generated from live market data — OI structure, PCR, institutional flows, global cues — not opinion or guesswork.

The analysis runs on a four-engine framework built on Upstox v2 API data: a Decision Engine (M2) gated by a four-pillar OI structure filter, a Wall Anticipator (M4) that confirms institutional positioning across two consecutive scan cycles, a Scalp Engine for intraday momentum plays, and a Monte Carlo simulator calibrated to current IV for strategy probability analysis.

All trade ideas are from a paper trading system running in real time — documented before live deployment. The system moves to live trading when win rate exceeds 45%, reward-to-risk exceeds 1.5, and max drawdown stays below 8% across 30+ trades.
